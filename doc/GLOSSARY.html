Glossary

This glossary isn't intended to demonstrate that I am Mr. Knowitall, I rather want to make sure that I use some common terms in a way commonly agreed upon. As I am (as you will already have realized) no native speaker of English, I am often unsure about how to exactly use a certain term. If you become aware of any misconceptions, please drop me a short note.
  • Builtin:
    I use this term to refer to functions and special forms implemented as C++ classes or functions.
